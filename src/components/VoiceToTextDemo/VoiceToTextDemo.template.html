How to Use Voice-to-Text

  • • Click the microphone button to start recording
  • • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • • Click the stop button or wait for automatic stop
  • • Your speech will be converted to text and added to the field
  • • Use continuous mode for longer descriptions
  • • Try different languages for better accuracy

Browser Requirements

  • • Requires HTTPS connection (for security)
  • • Supported in Chrome, Edge, and Safari
  • • Microphone permission must be granted
  • • Works best with a good internet connection
